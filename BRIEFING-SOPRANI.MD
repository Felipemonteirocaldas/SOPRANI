Soprani Engineering – Website
Development Brief
1. Company Overview
Soprani Engineering operates internationally in the metal packaging sector.
The company supports manufacturers with machinery sourcing, spare parts supply,
technical assistance, revamping projects and trading of tinplate and aluminum.
The website should communicate reliability, technical competence and
international industrial experience.

2. Website Objective
Create a professional website presenting Soprani Engineering as a reliable partner
for metal packaging companies worldwide. The site should generate business contacts,
quote requests and partnerships.

3. Target Audience
Metal packaging manufacturers
Can-making factories
Industrial buyers
Maintenance and technical managers
Machinery traders
Industrial investors
Suppliers in the metal packaging sector
Main geographic focus:
Europe, Middle East, North Africa, Asia and Americas.
